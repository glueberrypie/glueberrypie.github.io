Sun Mengtian works in installation and sound. She is interested in creative coding, knitting, digital painting and hardware hacking, and often uses the method of assemblage to create new works. Her work explores the relationship between digital technology and memory.

For a full CV, please get in contact via email. For curiosity, follow instagram.

We_Were_Once_Happy_MFASHOW_2025_HongKong

We Were Once Happy is a series of small kinetic installations, assembled with video clips and sound elements to create a memory space that is contradictory and cyclical. The work explores the opposing qualities in materials, movement and colors.

Coffee can, Fish can, Tape, Aluminum spiral, Vibration speaker, Relay, Wires, Billiards, Electromagnetic

2025

Hunt_2025_PRÉCÉDÉE_HongKong_(collaborate with artist Tung Wing Hong)

A continuous drawing recorded on a programmed moving canvas with sound effects according to cursor coordination. With the special stroke characteristic, the artist played with the canvas moving speed and direction to draw and erase, presenting a continuous narrative and transformation of visual and sound.
